FAQ

Are you already operating?

No, we are just starting this up. To make this a success and build a great platform, we would love your input. Please get in touch and join our Slack group or just fill out the questionnaire to show your support. It’s important to get to a critical mass of people so we know that we should go and fully build this.

Where will my money go?

97% of your money will go to charities that support your cause areas in the an effective way. The remaining 3% go to Stripe as a processing fee. This will change as soon as 'Give in Public' is registered as an official charity. To make that happen, we need your support. Help us start the 'Give in Public' movement.

How do you curate effective charities?

There are multiple meta organizations that assess the effectiveness of charities including givewell.org or founderspledge.com. We will use their recommendations as a reference.

How can I verify my donations?

We will provide you with a receipt for each donation that we make on your behalf. You can use this to verify the legitimacy and to deduct it from your tax.

Who is behind 'Give in Public'?

Hi, I am Laurin. After working for a tech company for the past years, I decided to take 12 months off to work on my own ideas. During these 12 months, I want to work on projects that contribute to a more equitable world. I believe that one way to achieve this goal is to better leverage technology in the non-profit sector. With ‘Give in Public’ the goal is to normalize giving and to multiply the impact everyone can have by making it public.


If you have some ideas or just want to connect, get in touch with me via Twitter or Email. Please also sign-up so I get a better understanding of what I need to build.
